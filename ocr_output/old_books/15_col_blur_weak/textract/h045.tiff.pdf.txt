VII. WILLIAM, son of Elijah Horton and Jemima Currie, born in
Stroudsburg, Pa., 14 May, *789; married at the old Judge Gore place,
in Sheshequin, 15 January, 1809, by Samuel Gore, Esq., to Estree
daughter of Christopher Cowell and Rachel Coolbough, and
born in Kingswrood, N. J., 19 January, He settled in 1811, in
Sheshequin, about two miles from the river, where his widow now
resides, She is now (1875) 87 years old, retains her mental faculties
without any apparent failure, and, excepting dimness of sight, her
bodily powers are remarkably well preserved. Her husband died
858. Her son, Isaac Jackson, with whom she lives very
comfortably, occupies the old homestead.
