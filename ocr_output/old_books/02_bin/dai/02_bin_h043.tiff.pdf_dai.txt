Peekskill, N. Y., 9 June, 1768; married in 1791, PAMELA OGDEN,
born in Northumberland, now Columbia Co., Pa., at the mouth of
Fishing Creek. Soon after the birth of their first child, they moved
up the Susquehanna River, and settled in Sheshequin. Pamela Ogden
died 11 February, 1804, aged forty years. He married, 2. ABIGAIL
BULLARD, born 11 April, 1780; died 3 March, 1845. He died
9 August, 1835. All buried in Horn Brook Cemetery.
