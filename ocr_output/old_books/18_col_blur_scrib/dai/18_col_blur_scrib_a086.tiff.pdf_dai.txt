(Only Begotten Descended)
Seat of the Supreme Patriarch. The foundation stone was led by St. Gregory the
Illuminator who built the Church in the third cent the Christian era.
The cathedral of Etchmiatzin is identified with Gregory; its name
"Etchmiatzin
in the Armenia the only begotten is
descended," a story attached to it is, that in a vision Christ appeared
to Gregory escended in light; Gregory built his church on the spot wire
the vision had appeared to him, giving it the name of "Etchmiatzin (only

Etchmiatzin, the ecclesiastical metropolis of Armenia.
