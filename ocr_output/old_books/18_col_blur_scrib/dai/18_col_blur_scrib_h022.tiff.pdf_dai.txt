THE HORTON NAME-FIRST-SETTLERS IN THIS COUNTRY
The word Horton in the Anglo-Saxon language means an enclosure,
or garden of vegetables. It is said to be derived from ort and fan,
ort, meaning plant, and fan, enclosed. The name is evidently of
Latin origin, and has been known in England ever since conquest
of Caesar
