And others had carssia that is found in a shallow
Inke guarded by fierce. bat-like creatures. To

with the hides of enttle, leaving openinga for their
cyes only. And there are the merchants who have
the ladanum that settles on low bushe, and that
sticks to the beards of he-goats that go amongst
the bushes. Others have the einnamen that is
used by birds to build their nests against high
cliffs. Men cannot climb these cliffa to gather the
sticks of consmon, but they make the birds bring
