THE OLD BIBLE
Perhaps the most interesting relic left by our good old ancestor,
Barnabus, is the OLD which he brought with him from England.
It is now in the possession of the Hon. Silas Horton, of Southold
It was taken to the Bible House, New York, some years ago, and care
fully repaired, and every torn leal mended, so that it is now entire,
and in a good condition.
We find in it the following record:
"Impriated at Londom, by the Depaties of Churistion Barker."
Printer to the Mout Exvellent Majentir,
Com
Then follows in manuscript
- Barmabes Hortion, to his som Jomathan Horton, this be did in
the your of our Lord, 1680."
Then follows:
- Cage. Jomatham Horton, bils Bond, Gad the him grays Hortom,
In another place, and proba to in handwriting of Jonathan
Horton, Jr., we find:
- The Ward of the Land Gal of 3
Again we find:
- Lydia Tv B her mother, as in is said."
And then 6 lows
- Feb. a. is w you 1742, them Jomatham Tuthill deported this life-the som of

Tuthill, departed this May 16. in the your 1743. la the goth your of her age.
