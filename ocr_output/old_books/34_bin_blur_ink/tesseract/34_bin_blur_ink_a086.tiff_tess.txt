(Only Begotten Dossended)
Seat of the Supreme Patfarch, The foundation stone was laid by St. Gregory the
Hfaminator who bast the Chureh ia the third certury of the Christian era
The cathedral of Etchmiatzin is identified with Gregory ; its name
‘“VEtchmiatzin’ means in the Armenian language ‘the only begotten is
descended,” and the story attached to itis, that ina vision Christ appeared
